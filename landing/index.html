FAQ

Common questions

Where does the data come from?

All contract data comes from USAspending.gov and FPDS—public federal procurement databases maintained by the U.S. government. We pull, clean, and analyze the data so you don't have to.

How is this different from GovWin?

GovWin is a search database. This is a curated weekly brief. You spend 8 minutes reading instead of 10 hours searching. Different tool, different job.

Can I see what the newsletter looks like?

Yes. View a full sample report from this week's data pull. What you see is exactly what lands in your inbox every Monday.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. One-click unsubscribe link at the bottom of every email. No hoops, no "are you sure?" screens, no guilt trips.

Who built this?

Luke Baek—a data engineer who got tired of watching small GovCon firms fly blind while the big shops hoarded intel behind $20K paywalls. Built this to level the playing field.
