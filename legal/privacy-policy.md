# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 18, 2026

## Introduction

GovCon Weekly Intelligence ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our newsletter, website, and related services (the "Service").

**Plain English Summary:** We collect minimal personal information (mainly your email address) to deliver our newsletter. We don't sell your data. We use standard analytics tools to improve our service. You can unsubscribe anytime.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Email Address (Required)**
When you subscribe to our newsletter through Beehiiv, we collect your email address. This is the primary personal information we collect.

**Optional Information**
You may choose to provide:
- Name or company name
- Company website
- Job title
- Industry or vertical interests

**Payment Information (Paid Subscriptions)**
For paid subscriptions, we collect billing information including:
- Name
- Billing address
- Payment card information (processed through our payment processor; we do not store full card numbers)

**Communications**
If you contact us (via email or contact forms), we collect the contents of your message and any information you choose to provide.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data**
When you visit our website or interact with our emails, we may automatically collect:
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Pages visited and time spent
- Referring website
- Email open rates and click-through rates

**Cookies and Tracking Technologies**
We use cookies and similar technologies to:
- Remember your preferences
- Analyze how you use our Service
- Measure the effectiveness of our content

See Section 7 for details on cookies and your choices.

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do **not** collect:
- Social Security numbers or government ID numbers
- Financial account numbers (except as needed for payment processing)
- Sensitive personal information (health data, biometric data, etc.)
- Information from children under 18

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or Switzerland, we process your personal information based on:

- **Contract Performance:** Processing necessary to deliver the Service you subscribed to

- **Legitimate Interests:** Processing necessary for our business operations, such as analytics and fraud prevention, balanced against your privacy rights
- **Legal Obligations:** Processing required to comply with applicable laws

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfill the purposes described in this policy:

- **After Unsubscribe:** We may retain your email address in a suppression list to honor your unsubscribe request and prevent re-adding you.

- **Analytics Data:** Aggregated and anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely.

You may request deletion of your personal information (see Section 10).

## 7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 7.1 What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize you and remember your preferences.

### 7.2 Types of Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies**
Required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled.
- Session cookies (expire when you close your browser)
- Authentication cookies (for paid subscriber accounts)

**Analytics Cookies**
Help us understand how visitors use our website.
- Page views, time on site, bounce rate
- Referral sources and navigation patterns

**Marketing Cookies** (optional)
Track your activity across websites for advertising purposes.
- Email campaign tracking pixels
- Third-party advertising cookies (if we use paid advertising)

### 7.3 Your Cookie Choices

**Browser Settings**
You can configure your browser to reject cookies or alert you when cookies are being set. Note that disabling essential cookies may impair website functionality.

**Opt-Out Tools**
- Google Analytics Opt-Out: [Browser Add-On](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- Do Not Track: We respect browser "Do Not Track" signals where technically feasible

**Email Tracking**
Most email clients allow you to disable automatic image loading, which prevents email tracking pixels from functioning.
