### Contractor Consolidation: The Big Are Getting Bigger

**13-week trend:** The top 10 contractors won 64% of total award value (vs. 52% in Q4 2025). The top 3 (GDIT, Leidos, Booz Allen) won 38% of total value.

**Prediction based on 13 weeks of data:** By end of FY26, the top 10 contractors will control 70%+ of federal IT spending. Mid-tier firms ($500M-$2B revenue) will get squeezed. They're too large to compete as small businesses, too small to compete as large primes.

**DO THIS:** If you're a mid-tier firm, you have three options: (1) Grow through M&A to compete with large primes, (2) Specialize in a niche where you can be #1 or #2, or (3) Position as the preferred teaming partner for large primes. Option 3 is the safest short-term play.
