## Outreach Best Practices

### Timing

| Channel | Best Day | Best Time | Open Rate |
|---------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| LinkedIn DM | Tue-Thu | 7-9am, 12-2pm ET | 40-60% |
| Cold Email | Tue-Wed | 6-8am, 4-6pm ET | 18-25% |
| Referral Ask | Thu-Fri | 10am-12pm ET | 35-45% |

### Personalization Checklist

Before sending ANY outreach, confirm you have:

- [ ] Recipient's correct first name (spelling matters)
- [ ] Company name (check if they rebrand or were acquired)
- [ ] Recent contract win or news mention (last 90 days)
- [ ] Specific vertical they focus on (from their website or LinkedIn)
- [ ] Name of 1-2 competitors they likely know
- [ ] Their current role/title (don't call a Director a VP)

### Response Handling

**If they reply "Not interested":**
```
Totally understand, [First Name]. Thanks for letting me know.

If anything changes or you want to see what we're up to down the road, you know where to find me.

Best of luck with [Company]'s growth.

— Luke
```

**If they reply "Send me a sample":**
```
You got it. Attached is last Friday's issue (March 15, 2026).

Key highlights:
- $67M in cybersecurity awards at DHS
- VA recompete alerts for Q3 2026
- Trend analysis: Why Zero Trust budgets are exploding

If you like it, subscribe here: [link]

If you have questions about what we track or how we source the data, happy to jump on a 15-min call.

— Luke
```

**If they reply with a question (e.g., "How much is the paid version?"):**
```
Great question, [First Name].

We're still finalizing pricing, but thinking $99-199/month depending on features.

Right now, we're focused on building the best possible product and getting feedback from early users like you.

Free trial is unlimited for now — sign up, read a few issues, and let me know what you think. When we launch paid tiers, I'll give you a founders discount (50% off first year).

Fair?

— Luke

P.S. — What features would you NEED to justify paying for this? I'm building the product roadmap right now and early input shapes the direction.
```

**If they ghost after initial interest:**
- Wait 7 days
- Send one more email: "Hey [First Name], saw you opened the last email but didn't reply. Still interested? If not, all good — just let me know so I don't keep bugging you."
- If still no response, move to long-term nurture (1 email/month with value, no ask)

## A/B Testing Plan

### Test 1: Subject Line Variants (LinkedIn DM)

**Control:** "Your competitors at NetCentrics just won $8M in Zero Trust work"

**Variant A:** "NetCentrics: $8M in new wins (should [Company] be worried?)"

**Variant B:** "Quick heads up: your competitors are moving fast in Zero Trust"

**Variant C:** "I track your competitors' contract wins — here's what I'm seeing"

**Hypothesis:** Direct threat + specificity (Control) will outperform vague warnings (B) and indirect approaches (C). But (A) may spike curiosity with the question format.

**Measure:** Open rate, reply rate, free signups

---

### Test 2: Email Length (Cold Email)

**Control:** 250 words (current Email 1 in Sequence 2)

**Variant A:** 100 words (ultra-short, just the core value prop + CTA)

**Variant B:** 400 words (longer story, more social proof, case study)

**Hypothesis:** GovCon BD professionals are busy. Shorter will perform better. But if they're in "research mode" for a solution, longer may build more credibility.

**Measure:** Open rate, click-through rate, reply rate

---

### Test 3: CTA Variants (All Channels)

**Control:** "Free signup: [link]"

**Variant A:** "Get your first issue Friday: [link]"

**Variant B:** "Join 47 other GovCon BD teams: [link]"

**Variant C:** "See what your competitors are reading: [link]"

**Hypothesis:** Social proof (B) or FOMO (C) will outperform generic "free signup" (Control). (A) adds specificity which may help.

**Measure:** Click-through rate, conversion rate

| **Open Rate** | 25%+ (email), 50%+ (LinkedIn) | Email tracking, LinkedIn read receipts |

| **Free Signup Rate** | 15% of replies | Beehiiv dashboard |
| **Paid Conversion** | 10% of free subs (after 30 days) | Stripe + manual tracking |

### Overall Sales Funnel

```
100 outreach attempts
  → 40 opens (40%)
    → 8 replies (20% of opens, 8% of sends)
      → 3 free signups (37.5% of replies, 3% of sends)
        → 1 paid conversion (33% of signups, 1% of sends)
```

**Result:** 1% send-to-paid conversion rate = need 100 sends/week to get 1 paid customer/week.

**Goal:** Improve to 2-3% conversion through testing, better targeting, and product improvements.
