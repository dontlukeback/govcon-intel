## CEO Directive

**To all departments (i.e., yourself):**

This is a **speed sprint**. Perfection is the enemy. The goal is not to build the perfect newsletter — it's to get real humans reading real intel and giving real feedback.

**Rules of engagement:**
- Ship daily. No "I'll finish it tomorrow."
- Quality bar: "good enough to be useful" not "good enough to win awards."
- If something takes more than 2 hours, cut scope or defer it.
- Respond to every subscriber personally in Week 1. Build relationships, not metrics.
- If you get stuck, ask for help (Claude, communities, mentors).

**Success looks like:**
- 100 people opted in to hear from you again
- 10 people replied to your newsletter with feedback
- 3 people shared it with colleagues
- 1 person asked "when can I pay for this?"

That's the bar. Hit it, then we level up.

Go.
